Object Detector

It captures real-time video from your webcam and highlights human presence with a bounding box, label, and confidence score. The detector follows a straightforward Computer Vision pipeline:

  1. Capture: Grabs a frame from the webcam.
  2. Transform: Resizes the image to the model's required input size (512 x 512).
  3. Inference: The custom PyTorch model (model.pth) processes the image.
  4. Display: If the confidence score is above 0.5, a bounding box is drawn around the detected person, with a label.
